However, these TGN (trunk line numbers) are used by the operators of the phone companies, and the service companies alike. Used mainly by the repair companies in reporting how a line comes in, it can be used by the intelligent as a means to social engineer various things from the phone company. Note that every area does have a TGN, but various locations are covered by another trunk lines. This is just a sampling of the multitude that is found within New Jersey at the time. Special business may have a different TGN. Also these TGN are based mainly from New Jersey Bell Phones. The other minor telephone companies will have different TGN for the same areas.
