Electronic Magazines: INFORMATIK

Informatik, the "Journal of Privileged Information", produced an excellent set of issues, each one packed with information and extremely well-written. The efforts to produce a a zine of this quality takes its toll and the editors mention as such towards the end. At least one of the editors, Rafe Colburn, has gone on to run an excellent site at rc3.org which is worth checking out. Interesting folks weigh in through the issues. Good work.
