The Conference Moderator is the Network Coordinator's designated representative. Moderators are the system operators of electronic telecommunications systems that are homes to ADAnet conferences. Duties of the Conference Moderator shall include: (a) administrate conferences and designate Conference Hosts, (b) maintainance as needed on conferences, (c) advise the Network Coordinator regarding conference problems. The Network Coordinator will appoint the Conference Moderator to serve at his pleasure, for any length of term. 2. Conference Hosts are designated by the Conference Moderator. Conference Hosts are responsible for their conferences and guide the discussion areas. Their duties include: (a) provide bulletins to nodes carrying their conference for the purpose of clarification and/ or information; (b) define what should and should not be discussion areas within each ADAnet conference; and (c) provide guidance to all concerned with legal responsibilities and/or disclaimers. The Host may be responsible for providing to the Conference Moderator a description of the conference, it's aims, and the scope of the conference. 3. The Conference Host for the Network Administration Conference, or any conference or device used by ADAnet as a administrative net-wide Electronic Mail and message distribution channel which services all nodes within ADAnet, is the Network Coordinator.
