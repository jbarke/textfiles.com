Category: Dips: Salsa, dips, etc... Recipe: FIESTA DIP Posted by: Dodger Quantity: ?? Ingredients: 2 cans Mexi-Corn (drained) 1 cup mayo (not salad dressing) 1 cup sour cream 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese several green onions Cayenne pepper to taste (I (shallots) chopped (about use 1-2 tsp. or more) 1/2 - 1 bunch) Salt to taste Instructions: Stir it all together and refrigerate... serve with tortilla chips or Fritos Comments: Del Monte has a version of mexi-corn, regular corn will do in a pinch but this with pepper bits is best Enjoy!
