Catagory: Breads Recipe: Banana bread Posted by: Bryn Quantity: ?? Ingredients: 1/2 c butter/marg 2/3 c sugar 2 eggs 1 and 3/4 c all purpose flour 1 tsp soda 1 c bananas 1/2 to 1 c nuts 1/2 tsp salt Instructions: Blend together butter/marg, sugar, and eggs Blend together in separate bowl all purpose flour, soda, salt Add to the butter/egg/sugar mix some of the flour, about 1/2 cup, then stir it all in. A wooden spoon works wonderfully Then add about 1/3 of the bananas, then stir them in. Repeat until flour and bananana are all added in, then add nuts. Bake at 350 F. for 20/30 mins, use the knife test to see if finished. Comments: 1 c bananas mooshed equals about 2 mediums, 1 1/2 large. (freeze them for about 2 days to get the degree of black awful nastiness you want) PS, don't melt the butter/marj, instead have it all room temp. Works best.
