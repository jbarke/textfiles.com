Quick and Easy Banana Nut Bread Category: Breads: Bread, muffins, rolls, etc... Posted by: Jolie Quantity: Ingredients: 1 c. sugar 1/2 c. butter/margarine 3 ripe bananas 2 eggs 2 c. flour 1 t. soda 2 t. milk 1/2 t. salt 1/4 c. nuts Instructions: Mix in order given. Put in a greased loaf pan. Bake at 325 for about 1 hr. Comments:
