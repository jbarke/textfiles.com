MAIL ORDER TICKET PROBLEMS.(Mon-Fri 11-5)............415-457-8034

Tickets for 1992 are $27 each, which includes the $2.50 processing fee.

Mail Order ticket instructions:

      Send a #10 size business envelope. On outside of envelope, list what
concert and how many tickets for each.  Also list your full name and address.
Inside the envelope must be the following:
      - A 3" x 5" index card with your name, address, and phone number in the
upper left-hand corner.  List how many tickets you want and for which concerts.
      - A return envelope (#10 size) with your name and address on it, and a
first class stamp (.29) for 1 to 10 tickets, two stamps for more than 10 tickets.
      - A American Express Money Order for the total amount, with your name and
address written on it.

      Send to:          Grateful Dead Ticket Sales - name of concert
                        Box CS 8150
                        San Rafael, CA 94912

Not responsible for lost, stolen tickets. Strongly suggest Registered mail.  If
you want Registered mail for your tickets, do not put any postage on the return
envelope and include an additional $6.40 per order.

Ticket limit 4 per person per show. Be sure your name and address are on the
outside of the envelope, the return envelope, money order, and the 3 x 5 card. 
If you are going to tape the show, write "Taper" on your order (limit 2). If you
will not accept behind stage or side stage seats, indicate this on your order.

Orders are accepted on a first-come basis according to the postmark. Postmarks
before the beginning mail order date will not be accepted.  Mail orders will be
accepted for Spring 1992 shows beginning January 8th.

Showtime is 7:30 pm for all shows.
