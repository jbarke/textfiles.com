  1)  No profanity in the message areas.

  2)  No flames -- No bashing other users for the silly things they say 
      and do.

  3)  No Illegal Information - Do not pass/post any illegal information 
      on RPGNet.  This includes but is not limited to phreak or hack 
      codes and software serial numbers or registration codes.

  4)  Stay on topic in the message areas.  SYSOPs should try to keep 
      thier users from posting off-topic messages.

  5)  No WARS.  There are no WAR boards on RPGNet.  

  6)  All RPGNet BBS's must accept CRASH mail.

  7)  All NODES should poll their respective HUB at least once per day. 
      Contact your HUB if you have some odd special case situation.

  8)  RPGNet BBS's must be 24-hour BBS's.  No part timers!  If your
      system is going down for longer than 72 hours for any reason, you
      must contact your HUB and be flagged as DOWN in the nodelist.

  9)  The RPG_ADMI area is for RPGNet SYSOPs, Co-SYSOPs and General
      Echomail Moderator use ONLY!

  10) Your tag lines must include your RPGNet node address. Do NOT use 
      any other address for your RPGNet bases.

  11) Do not cross-post RPGNet messages to other networks.

  12) Do not feed RPGNet File Echos or Echomail to non-RPGNet BBS's.  
      Doing so will result in a WARNING, and possible removal from 
      RPGNet.

  13) SYSOPs are directly responsible for the messages posted by users 
      from their BBS.  Please watch your message bases.

  14) RPGNet SYSOPs should make the RPGAPP.* archive available to all 
      users.

  15) All RPGNet Systems should display the RPGNet Logo somewhere on 
      their BBS.  Show that you are proud of the Networks you carry!
